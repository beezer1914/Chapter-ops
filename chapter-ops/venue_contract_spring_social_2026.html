4. Terms and Conditions

4.1 Cancellation Policy.

Cancellation more than 30 days prior to the event date will result in forfeiture of the deposit only. Cancellation within 30 days of the event date will result in forfeiture of 75% of the total contract value. Cancellation within 7 days of the event date will result in forfeiture of 100% of the total contract value.

4.2 Damage and Liability.

The Licensee is responsible for any damage to the venue or its property caused by the Licensee, its guests, vendors, or contractors. A refundable damage deposit of $500.00 will be collected at the time of balance payment and returned within 14 business days following the event, less any documented damage costs.

The Licensee must obtain a temporary special event license from the Georgia Department of Revenue if alcohol will be served. The Licensor does not provide bar services under this agreement. The Licensee assumes full liability for alcohol-related incidents on the premises.

4.4 Capacity and Safety.

Attendance may not exceed the stated maximum of 120 guests in accordance with applicable fire and safety codes. The Licensor reserves the right to halt the event if capacity limits are exceeded or if the safety of guests or staff is at risk.

4.5 Outside Vendors.

The Licensee may bring outside caterers, DJs, photographers, and decorators with 7 days' prior written notice to the Licensor. All outside vendors must carry general liability insurance with a minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence limit and name Harrington Event Center LLC as an additional insured.
