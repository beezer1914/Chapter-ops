ChapterOps

A converged chapter operations platform built specifically for Greek letter organizations. ChapterOps replaces the fragmented patchwork of disconnected tools most chapters rely on — bringing member management, dues collection, intake pipelines, events, communications, documents, and lineage tracking into a single platform.

  • Member management & chapter transfers
  • Stripe-powered dues collection & payment plans
  • Intake & MIP pipeline management
  • Events, communications & document vault
  • Lineage tracking & chapter history
  • Regional dashboards & multi-chapter oversight

About

Built by people who know the problem.

Blue Column Systems was founded in Georgia with a simple belief: the best software is built by people who have lived the problem firsthand. We don't adapt generic tools for niche communities — we build from the ground up with deep domain knowledge baked in from day one.

Our team brings together software engineering and insider knowledge of the organizations we serve, allowing us to build platforms that feel native to their users rather than foreign impositions.

01

Domain First

We don't start with technology. We start with a deep understanding of the community, its culture, and the real problems its members face day to day.

02

Convergence Over Complexity

Every product we build aims to reduce the number of tools an organization needs — not add to the pile. One platform that works is worth ten that almost work.

03

Built to Last

We build with enterprise-grade architecture from day one — multi-tenant isolation, role-based access, and security practices that scale with our customers.
